Recent projects

Some recent projects carried out in my spare time. Most work is open sourced and available on my Github.

Gower Tides

View on Google Play

An Android app for displaying tidal and weather data for the Gower Peninsula in South Wales. Differentiates from other similar tide applications since the calculations are made offline, which is useful due to limited data services in some areas of the Gower.

UWC Apply

View project

A web application allowing students to register and apply to UWC colleges. Provides a UCAS-style application system where applicants can complete various parts of the application, view their progress pre- and post-submission, pay the application fee, view internal messages, and so on.

UK Crime Mashup

View source

View project

Completed as part of a 2-day social-science hackathon with Martin Chorley and Matt Williams. A web application that attempts to visually find correlations between crime intensity or crime outcome intensity and the food hygeine rating of the area.
