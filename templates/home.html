Hi, I'm Will

I'm an engineer helping to build and grow Simply Do Ideas, which provides a platform helping young people to understand and realise their entrepreneurial ideas.

Before joining Simply Do Ideas, I was a software engineer at Chaser, and before that I was a researcher at Cardiff University's School of Computer Science & Informatics, where I completed my PhD (looking into the 'unfiltered feed' problem in online social networks) and moved on to join the UK MoD and US Army Research Labs coalition-led ITA project.

Whilst at Cardiff University, I also lectured the Advanced Computer Science MSc module Web & Social Computing and the Computer Science BSc module Human-Computer Interaction.
