Hi, I'm Will

I'm currently a research associate at the School of Computer Science & Informatics at Cardiff University in the UK.

I am co-coordinator of the MobiSoc forum, of which the main focus is around internet, mobile, and social computing. Most of my past and current research is associated with these domains.

My PhD research addressed the "unfiltered feed problem" by helping with identifying interesting content on Twitter.

I am currently lecturing web and social computing (MSc Advanced Computer Science) and human-computer interaction and algorithms & data structures (BSc Computer Science). The course material for these modules is available on GitHub (CMT111, CM2101, and CM2303).
