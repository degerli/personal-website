PhD research

Inferring Interestingness in Online Social Networks

I achieved my PhD from the School of Computer Science & Informatics, Cardiff University.

This research was to do with the dissemination of content and information in online social networks - in particular, Twitter - for the purposes of identifying and ranking the interestingness of information contributed by users. I was supervised by Dr Stuart Allen and Prof Roger Whitaker.
